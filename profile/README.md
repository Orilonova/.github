# **Orilonova — Research Organisation**

Orilonova is a small, low‑profile virtual organisation focused on exploratory research and conceptual development.  
The GitHub space serves as a **neutral container** for internal experiments, prototypes, and documentation.

---

## 📁 **Repositories**
- **Internal Notes** — Lightweight documentation and scratch‑work.  
- **Experiments** — Prototypes, tests, and exploratory models.  
- **Tools** — Utility scripts and internal helpers.  
- **Archive** — Historical snapshots and deprecated materials.

---

## 🔒 **Research Policy**
Orilonova maintains a strict separation between:

- Public infrastructure  
- Private conceptual work  
- Protected intellectual property  

Only **non‑sensitive scaffolding** is published here.

---

## 🧭 **Purpose of This Organisation**
The GitHub organisation exists to:

- Coordinate internal development  
- Store neutral technical assets  
- Maintain version control  
- Support future modular expansion  

It does **not** describe the underlying research directions.

---

## 🤝 **Contributions**
This organisation is currently closed to external contributions.  
Public issues and discussions are intentionally limited.

---

## 📜 **Licensing**
Licensing is applied per‑repository and may vary.  
Most materials are internal and not intended for external use.

---

## 🪶 **Design Philosophy**
Orilonova follows a **low‑profile, minimal‑disclosure** approach.  
Only operational scaffolding is visible; conceptual content remains private.

---

## 🔮 **Long‑Term Vision**
The organisation will gradually evolve as internal research matures.  
Public visibility will remain intentionally limited.
